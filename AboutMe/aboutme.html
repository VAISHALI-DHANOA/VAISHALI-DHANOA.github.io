I am a Postdoctoral Researcher working with Aarhus University and TU Wien. Due to work and family, I divide my time among Denmark, Austria, and India.

When I am not traveling, I enjoy biking and petting street cats wherever I go.

My research interests lie in data visualization, storytelling with data, and human-centered AI. We are currently living in a particularly interesting moment, where AI is beginning to fundamentally reshape data analysis practices. I am especially interested in understanding how these changes will influence the way we work with data.

Consequently, most of my research focuses on the future of data visualization in the age of AI.
